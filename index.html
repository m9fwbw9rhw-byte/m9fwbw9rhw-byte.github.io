About Grapevine Garden

Welcome to Grapevine Garden.

This channel follows a remarkable greenhouse grapevine, beautiful roses and the changing seasons of an English garden.

Enjoy relaxing videos, garden tours and updates from one of the garden's most treasured features: a mature grapevine that has been growing for over 50 years.

The 50-Year-Old Grapevine

The heart of Grapevine Garden is a mature grapevine that fills the greenhouse with lush growth and abundant harvests each year. Follow its journey through the seasons and discover how one vine can transform an entire greenhouse.
